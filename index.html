In our second semester we had a course in which we got to know unity. For our final project we had to create a game like environment using unity.

For the game I could reuse much of the knowledge from the group project I made in the same time period (the next project) and expand my skills concerning UI design. I tried to design it similar to a computer since the playable character is an agent fulfilling their job.

The idea: The city has a strict color palette: white, grey and pink. Your goal is to identify and defeat enemies that break that rule.

By the end I learned that I used up way too much time building the city in blender which then resullted in me working day and night on the integration with unity and the programming of the game's mechanics. But the result is quite pretty and maybe I will flesh out the game further in the future.

Programs: Blender for modelling, Unity for programming and UI

As part of our final project for a second semester course, we had to participate as a group in the uni's one week game jam.

The idea: You play an introvert called Blair. You take care of daily tasks over the course of a week and must keep an eye on your social battery. The goal is to preserve as much energy as possible by avoiding any social interaction.

As a group of five we divided the work among us rather quickly, and I wanted to focus on programming so I made the code for the game's mechanics.

My goal was to practice C-sharp and I managed to face the various troubles and problems on my way with the help of our lecturer, my notes from the course and some online forums. I also made the trailer at the end of this section. I used some gameplay I recorded, added subtitles and added some music that was part of our game.

In the end I am happy I could learn a lot from this game jam and the end result was a quite playable game which is very great as our first.

Programs: Unity for programming, Premiere Pro for Trailer editing
